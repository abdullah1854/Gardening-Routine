Help & Reference

1
Mustard Cake

Apply to DRY soil, then water. Ferment 48-72hrs, dilute 1:10

2
Neem Oil & Btk

Evening only - prevents leaf burn, protects bees

3
Timing Gap

Keep 10-15 days gap between Mustard Cake & Neem Khali

4
Iron Conflicts

Iron + Phosphorus/Magnesium = bad. Keep 7-10 day gaps

!
Carbendazim Warning

KILLS beneficial microbes (12% compatible). 14+ day gap mandatory

5
Bio-Agents

Alternate Paecilomyces & Trichoderma. Never same day

6
Chemical Fungicide

PPE required, avoid blooms, 14-day gap from bio-agents

7
Seaweed Extract

Keep 4-5 day gap from chemical fertilizers

Soil Health

28d Trichoderma/Paecilomyces - Alternate, moist soil
20d Pseudomonas Fluorescent - 5g/L soil drench
21-28d Epsom Salt & Iron - Stagger by 7 days
365d Wood Ash - Winter application only

Use With Caution

45d max Saaf Fungicide - Disease only, 14d+ from bio-agents
